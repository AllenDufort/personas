Conclusion

My main takeaway from this project is that you should always consider the user when designing a product. Creating personas and storyboards helped me understand the needs, wants and feelings of stakeholders and how to change the design to potentially improve user experience. Getting feedback from real users also helped me realize how much users value a simple and easy-to-use interface. My last takeaway is that you should not assume that your product is intuitive to users, even if the product is commonly used.
