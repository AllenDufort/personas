Project Overview

Do you remember the first time you used a microwave? Personally, I don't, but I do remember the first time I used the new microwave on the 4th floor of the CIT. I was confused and could not figure out how to open it when my food was done heating.

This confusion inspired me to figure out if other people had a similar experience and how I could change that. I then decided to observe and survey 3 people to learn about their experiences using the microwave. After collecting the survey results and observations, I created 2 personas to represent different types of users may interact with the CIT microwave. Finally, I created a storyboard to illustrate the experience of using the microwave from beginning to end.

This entire process helped me understand the needs, wants and feeling of the microwave users and how to change the design to potentially improve user exeperiece.
